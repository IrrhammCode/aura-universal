# 🧠 Aura Autonomous Platform: Official Knowledge Matrix

## 1. Overview
Aura is a high-performance, autonomous AI agent platform designed for the next generation of customer success. It leverages ultra-low latency inference to provide human-like interactions across text, voice, and visual channels.

## 2. Core Infrastructure (The Neural Stack)
- **Neural Reasoning**: Powered by Groq (Llama 3.3-70b). This engine provides near-instant decision making (12ms latency).
- **Vocal Synthesis**: Powered by ElevenLabs. Supports over 29 languages with emotional depth and professional resonance.
- **Visual Synthesis**: Powered by HeyGen. Provides real-time streaming avatars for face-to-face AI communication.
- **Vision Perception**: Powered by Fal.ai. Allows agents to "see" and analyze images sent by customers in real-time.

## 3. Product Features & Pricing
- **Fleet Command**: Manage multiple autonomous agents from a single dashboard.
- **Knowledge Matrix**: Ingest website data, documents, and PDFs to give your agents a long-term memory.
- **Human Takeover**: Real-time monitoring of conversations with the ability for human agents to pause the AI and intervene.
- **Pricing Plans**:
    - **Starter**: Free (up to 1,000 interactions/month).
    - **Professional**: $99/month (Unlimited interactions, Custom Avatars).
    - **Enterprise**: Custom pricing (SLA, Dedicated Neural Channels).

## 4. Troubleshooting & Support
- **Neural Sync Issues**: If indicators are red, click the "Neural Sync" button in the sidebar.
- **Voice Delay**: Ensure your ElevenLabs API quota is sufficient.
- **Lead Capture**: All leads are automatically synced to the "Neural Traces" and "Inbox" sections.
